About Us

Who we are

INTELLITECH is an information Technology Company, focused on providing optional, Comtemporary and Prospective ICT solutions in the World.

INTELLITECH focuses on the following core areas; Sales of Computer Systems, Computer accessories, and gadgets; as well as configuring of Servers, Training, Consulting, Providing maintenance of Systems and Softwares.

INTELLITECH was incorporated in 2017, and has grown from a humble beginning to a renowned ICT solutions provider in Nigeria today, with nation-wide presence, and a branch network that include the cities of Lagos, Abuje, PortHarcourt.
